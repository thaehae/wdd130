Overview

Purpose

The modern woman works, studies and raises her children. She is fast paced multi-tasker who is up-to-date on fashion for herself and for her children. The purpose of this website is to cater for those working moms who don't have time to walk through malls. They can walk through this website and discover an easy way to purchase original handprinted pacific styled clothing. This website will portray a warm, tranquil relaxing feeling like you are on the Islands to help moms feel relaxed as they shop. This website will provide an easy, no fuss, simple way to purchase, be easy to navigate, be inviting with some colorful images plus neutral tones and provide updated testimonials from satisfied customers.

Audience

The target audience of this website is working moms with children aged from birth to teens. These are working parents who want to dress their children in beautiful pacific styled clothing that reminds them of their pacific heritage and their home life back on the islands. The bright, vibrant, hand printed clothing will appeal to all cultures. The 'teuila' flower is the national flower of Samoa reminds us of the beauty of the islands, the beauty of women and children everywhere. This site will be accessed on laptops and mobiles showcasing bright, vibrant colours and readable fonts. This website will reflect the peace and tranquility of the islands.

Choose from a variety of Island childrens wear. Teuila (pronounced Tear-oo-ee-lar) is the national flower of Samoa. While travelling to the beautiful Samoa Islands you will notice the spectacular vibrant red of the Teuila flower in bloom. Teuila means red ginger of the Samoan ginger plant. Near pristine shades of blue oceans and white warm sand, the Teuila is surrounded by fresh green foliage and black volcanic rock. These colours are captured in the handprinted fabrics at TKK.
